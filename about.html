About

The idea for Vick's came in late February of the year 1994 when Victoria came up with the idea
open her own restaurant. The first Vick's was opened in Columbus, Ohio but has sense opened
new locations in N.Y.C. and L.A. and San Francisco.

Victoria decided to pay homage to her own mother, Treasa, with the idea for her authentic
Italian cusine.
